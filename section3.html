Other Versions of Taskmaster


Taskmaster has several other content spinoffs, including a board game, two books, an app, several localized programs, and a junior spinoff announced in March 2023. Countries where the show has been localized include: Sweden, the United States, Denmark, Norway, Finland, New Zealand, and Australia. Tasks are also given to the show's fans to complete at home. _Taskmaster_ also has special episodes for each New Year's Day and for every five series.
